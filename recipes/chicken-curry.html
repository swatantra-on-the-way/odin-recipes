Chicken Curry

Description:

Chicken curry or curry/curried chicken is a South Asian dish originating from India. It is common in the Indian subcontinent, Caribbean, Arabian Peninsula, Southeast Asia, Great Britain, and Japan.

Indian cuisine has a large amount of regional variation, with many variations on the basic chicken curry recipe. Indian chicken curry typically starts with whole spices, heated in oil.

Ingredients:

  • Curry powder
  • Garlic, ginger, and onion
  • Chicken broth/stock and coconut milk
  • Chicken
  • Peas
  • Coriander/cilantro
